Movement as the
Foundation of Healing

Physiotherapy is one of the most versatile and impactful disciplines in modern healthcare — addressing the full range of conditions that limit movement, cause pain, and prevent people from functioning at their best. Ms. Rina Valentin has built her practice on this belief: that skilled, compassionate physiotherapy can restore quality of life in ways that medication alone cannot.

Based at Lindequist Street in Windhoek, Ms. Valentin provides comprehensive physiotherapy care for patients of all ages and activity levels. Her scope extends from post-operative rehabilitation and sports injuries to chronic pain management and general musculoskeletal conditions — treating each patient as an individual with a unique set of goals.

"The body has a remarkable capacity to heal — but it needs the right guidance, the right technique, and the right timing."

— Ms. Rina Valentin

Her clinical approach integrates manual therapy and dry needling with progressive exercise therapy — combining hands-on techniques that address the source of dysfunction with active rehabilitation strategies that build strength, stability, and lasting recovery.

Ms. Valentin welcomes direct bookings from patients as well as referrals from general practitioners, orthopaedic surgeons, and sports medicine physicians across Windhoek and Namibia.
