Services

Care That Gets You Moving Again

Comprehensive physiotherapy care tailored to your individual needs — from acute sports injuries and surgical recovery to chronic pain and long-term musculoskeletal health. Ms. Valentin brings clinical precision and genuine care to every consultation.

General Physiotherapy

Comprehensive assessment and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, movement disorders, and physical dysfunction across all ages and activity levels.

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Targeted rehabilitation for joint, muscle, ligament, and bone conditions — restoring full range of motion, strength, and functional capacity.

Sports Injuries

Expert management of acute and chronic sports injuries — from sprains and strains to tendinopathies and overuse conditions — with a focus on safe return to sport.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programs following orthopaedic and other surgical procedures — ensuring safe, progressive restoration of function and independence.

Manual Therapy & Dry Needling

Hands-on joint mobilisation, soft tissue techniques, and dry needling to reduce pain, release trigger points, and restore normal movement patterns.

Pain Management & Injury Prevention

Evidence-based strategies for chronic and acute pain, combined with personalised exercise therapy and education to prevent future injury.
